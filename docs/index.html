Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Book Identifier ("we", "our", or "the extension") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our Chrome extension. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being fully transparent about our data practices.

1. What Data We Collect

Book Identifier collects only the minimum data necessary to function:

  • Website Content (Screen Region Image): When you initiate a book lookup by selecting a region of your screen, that selected area is captured as an image. This image is sent to our backend server solely to extract the book title or cover text using AI. The image is processed in real time and is never stored, logged, or retained after processing.
  • Device ID: A randomly generated anonymous identifier is created the first time you use the extension and stored locally in your browser. This ID is used only for rate limiting (to prevent abuse) and is never linked to your identity, account, or any personal information.
  • User Preferences: Your backend URL setting (if customized) is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. This never leaves your device except as the destination for API requests.

We do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information
  • Your browsing history or the URLs of pages you visit
  • Your location
  • Any data from pages you visit beyond the screen region you explicitly select
  • Authentication credentials or payment information

2. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Book title extraction: The screen region image you select is sent to our backend and processed by the Anthropic Claude AI API to identify the book title or author from the image.
  • Book information retrieval: The extracted title is used to query third-party book data services to retrieve ratings, genres, plot summary, and reader reviews.
  • Rate limiting: Your anonymous device ID is used to enforce a daily usage limit and prevent abuse of the service.
Important: We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, analytics beyond basic usage counts, or any purpose other than providing the book lookup service.

3. Data Sharing — Third Parties

To provide the book lookup service, your screen region image and extracted book title may be shared with the following third parties:

  • Anthropic (Claude API): The selected screen region image is sent to Anthropic's API for AI-based text extraction. Anthropic's privacy policy applies to this processing. View Anthropic's Privacy Policy →
  • RapidAPI / Goodreads Data Provider: The extracted book title is sent to a Goodreads data API via RapidAPI to retrieve ratings and reviews. No image or personal data is shared with this service. View RapidAPI's Privacy Policy →
  • Searlo Search API: The extracted book title is sent to Searlo to locate the correct Goodreads book ID. No image or personal data is shared. Searlo website →

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any other third parties for marketing, advertising, or any other purpose.

4. Data Retention

  • Screen region images: Never stored. Discarded immediately after title extraction.
  • Book lookup results: Cached in our database to improve performance for future lookups of the same book. Cached data contains only book metadata (title, rating, genres, etc.) — never any user-identifying information.
  • Device ID and preferences: Stored locally in your browser. Cleared when you uninstall the extension.

5. Data Security

All communication between the extension and our backend server is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. We do not store raw images or any sensitive user data on our servers.

6. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personally identifiable information, there is no personal data profile to access, correct, or delete. If you wish to reset your anonymous device ID, you can do so by uninstalling and reinstalling the extension.

7. Children's Privacy

Book Identifier is not directed at children under the age of 13 and we do not knowingly collect any data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
